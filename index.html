Your Privacy Is Our Priority

We understand that your conversations are personal and meaningful. That's why we've designed our process with your privacy as the absolute priority:

  • Automated Processing: Your chats are analyzed by our secure algorithm only—never read by human eyes.
  • Encrypted Upload: All data is transmitted using end-to-end encryption protocols.
  • Automatic Deletion: Your raw conversation data is permanently deleted after your Love Chart is created.
  • No Data Retention: We only keep the visualization data needed to create your chart, not the actual messages.

We believe beautiful memories shouldn't compromise your privacy.

How to Access Your Chat History

Follow these simple steps to export your conversations and start creating your Love Chart. Select your platform and device to see specific instructions.

Open the Conversation

Open WhatsApp and navigate to the chat you want to export.

Access Menu

Tap the three dots in the top-right corner to open the menu.

Select "More"

In the dropdown menu, tap on "More" options.

Export Chat

Select "Export chat" from the options.

Choose "Without Media"

When asked if you want to include media, select "Without Media" (we only need the text).

Share the File

Choose to share via email or save to your device, then upload the file to our website.

Note: The exported file will be in .txt format. Our system will automatically process this to create your Love Chart.

Open the Conversation

Open WhatsApp and go to the chat you want to export.

View Contact Info

Tap on the contact's name at the top of the screen.

Scroll Down

Scroll to the bottom of the contact info screen.

Export Chat

Tap on "Export Chat" option.

Choose "Without Media"

Select "Without Media" when prompted (we only need the text conversation).

Share the File

Use the iOS share sheet to save the file to Files, email it to yourself, or directly upload it to our website.

Note: The export process might take a moment for longer conversations. Be patient and don't close WhatsApp during this process.

Open WhatsApp on Your PC

Go to web.whatsapp.com or use the WhatsApp Desktop app. Log in by scanning the QR code using your phone.

Select the Chat to Export

Click on the chat you want to export. Make sure it is fully loaded by scrolling to the top so that all messages are visible if needed.

Open Chat Settings

Click on the contact name at the top of the chat window to open chat settings. Scroll down and select Export Chat (only available in the mobile app).

Use Your Phone to Export

Since exporting is only possible via mobile, open the WhatsApp app on your phone → open the same chat → tap the three dots (⋮) → More → Export Chat. Choose Without Media for faster processing.

Transfer Exported File to PC

WhatsApp will generate a .txt file and give you options to send it via email or save to Google Drive. Download this file on your PC.

Upload the Chat File

Once downloaded, upload the exported .txt file directly to our platform. We'll analyze it and generate your report.

Do not include media files. They are not required for our analysis and will only slow down the upload.

Open Instagram

Go to the chat you want to export.

Go to Instagram Settings

Open the Instagram app, tap your profile icon, then tap the three lines (☰) in the top-right corner. Go to Settings & Privacy → Accounts Center → Your Information and Permissions → Download Your Information.

Request Data Export

Select the Instagram account you want to export data from. Under "Information format", choose JSON. Under "Select information", tap Messages only, and choose the date range if needed.

Download the File

After submitting the request, Meta will prepare your data. You’ll get a download link via email or in the app within a few minutes to a few hours. Download the ZIP file and extract it.

Upload the Chat File

Inside the extracted folder, look for messages/inbox/ and find the chat folder you want. Upload the message_1.json file to our platform. We'll do the rest.

Make sure to select JSON format, not HTML, for better compatibility with our analysis system.

Go to Instagram

Open the app and navigate to the conversation.

Go to Instagram Settings

Open the Instagram app, tap your profile icon, then tap the three lines (☰) in the top-right corner. Navigate to Settings & Privacy → Accounts Center → Your Information and Permissions → Download Your Information.

Request Data Export

Select the Instagram account to export from. Choose JSON under “Information format”. Tap Select information, choose Messages only, and set a custom date range if needed.

Download the File

After confirming your request, Meta will send you a download link via email or in the app. This can take a few minutes to a few hours. Download the ZIP file to your iPhone or transfer it to a PC.

Upload the Chat File

Unzip the file. Inside the messages/inbox/ folder, find the chat folder with the name of your contact. Upload the message_1.json file to our platform for analysis.

Always select the JSON format for full compatibility with our system.

Open Instagram Web

Go to instagram.com and login.

Open Instagram on Browser

Go to instagram.com and log into your account. Click your profile icon, then go to Settings → Accounts Center.

Access Data Download

Inside the Accounts Center, click Your Information and Permissions → Download Your Information. Select your Instagram account, then choose JSON format.

Select Only Messages

Click Select Information and choose only Messages. You can also set a date range if you want specific chats only.

Download and Extract

After submitting, Meta will notify you via email or browser when your file is ready. Download and unzip the folder. Navigate to messages/inbox/ and find the conversation folder you want.

Upload JSON File

Inside the chat folder, upload the message_1.json file to our site. We'll take it from there.

Make sure to choose JSON format, not HTML, or the system won't work properly.
